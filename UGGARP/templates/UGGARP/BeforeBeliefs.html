Procedure


In the next section you will be shown the parts of the tables of potential Proposal Rules. You will be asked what do you think is the percent chance that each of the displayed Proposal Rule would be Rejected by the Responder. Please provide the number between 0 and 100-- the percentage chance with which you think the Responder would Reject the Proposal Rule.
In total you will make 12 predictions for different proposal rules. One of them will be chosen determine your payment. Your payment in this round will depend on your decisions and a chance.

Payment


You will be able to earn up to $10 in addition to your earnings in the decision stage. You will receive the additional payment if you would be able to outperform the random machine in predicting the behavior other participants in the room. The earnings will be according to the following procedure, after you submit all predictions :

  1. One of the proposal rules is chosen at random
  2. One participant from the people participated in the session (excluding You) is picked at random and her decision is retrieved
  3. Your error is computed as (prediction/100)2 if the decision picked is Accept or (1-prediction/100)2 if the decision picked is Reject
  4. The uniform random number is drawn form the interval from 0 to 1
  5. You $10 (in addition to money you already earned) if your error is below random number and do not receive any additional payment otherwise.


Examples


Example One: Assume Your prediction was $70\%$ that a Responder will Reject the offer. The randomly retrieved decision was Reject. Hence, your error is (1-70/100)2=.32=.09. Then, if random number (uniformly distributed from $0$ to $1$) is above $.09$ you will get $10, otherwise (random number is below than .09) you will not get additional payment.

Example Two: Assume Your prediction was $40\%$ that a Responder will Reject the offer. The randomly retrieved decision was Accept. Hence, your error is (40/100)2=.42=.16. Then, if random number (uniformly distributed from $0$ to $1$) is above $.16$ you will get $10, otherwise (random number is below than .16) you will not get additional payment. {%endblock%}
